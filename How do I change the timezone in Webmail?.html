Webmail defaults to the server's timezone. You can change it to your local time if you like by following the steps described in the following sections.

SquirrelMail

  1. Log into Webmail for your domain.
  2. On the top left click 'Settings'.
  3. Click the 'Personal Information' option.
  4. From the dropdown menu titled 'Your current timezone', choose your local timezone.
  5. Click the Submit button on the lower right to save your changes.
