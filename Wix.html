Wix.com is a cloud-based web development platform that allows users to create HTML5 websites and mobile sites, through the use of their online drag and drop tools.

You can read more about their services at wix.com.

Pointing your DreamHost domain to Wix

To point your domain from DreamHost to Wix, you must change your DNS settings. The best option is to point your nameservers to Wix.

Visit the following article which describes how to adjust your nameservers for a domain registered at DreamHost:

  • Nameservers

When you're on the edit page, change your nameservers to the following:

  • NS1.WIX.COM
  • NS2.WIX.COM

Once you have updated your nameservers, it may take 72 hrs to propagate online. You can check the status of your DNS changes on the following site:

  • https://www.whatsmydns.net/

Configuring your domain at Wix

The final step is to then add your domain to Wix. Visit the following article for further instructions:

  • Configuring your domain at Wix

Once the nameservers have propagated online and your site is configured at Wix, your site is ready to be hosted on their servers.
