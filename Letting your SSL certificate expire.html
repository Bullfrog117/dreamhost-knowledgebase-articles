If you do not wish to renew a certificate, you can set it to 'Let Expire'.

  1. Navigate to the (Panel > 'Domains' > 'Secure Hosting') page.
  2. Click the Settings button to the right of the domain.
  3. On this page click the Let Expire button.

When the term for the certificate has expired your website will no longer show a secure lock when visiting HTTPS. Only expire a certificate if you no longer need to secure your website.
