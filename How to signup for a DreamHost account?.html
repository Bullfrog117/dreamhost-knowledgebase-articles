This article explains how to sign up for a new DreamHost account. This is for new customers that are new to DreamHost and wish to open an account with their personal details and payment information.

  1. Visit dreamhost.com.
  2. On the top navigation menu, click 'All Services' to view a list of services DreamHost offers. Or navigate the website to find which service is best for your needs.
  3. Click the service you wish to purchase. In this example 'Shared Hosting' is chosen.
  4. On the service page, click the button to sign up for the service.
  5. After clicking the buttton, you're taken to signup.dreamhost.com. In this example it opens https://signup.dreamhost.com/shared/email/.
  6. Enter an email and password.
  7. On the next page you can choose to register a new free domain, add a domain you already own, start with a free dreamhosters.com subdomain, or add a domain later.
  8. Choose a billing option.
  9. Select if you wish to add a Private MySQL server to your account.
  10. The final page asks you to enter your personal information and payment details.
  11. Click the Place order now button at the bottom to complete the signup process.

If you have any further questions, please contact DreamHost technical support
