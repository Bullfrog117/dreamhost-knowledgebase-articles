To preview your site before you publish it to a domain name:

  1. Click the Publish button in the upper toolbar.
    An animated rocket appears showing you the upload progress of your website:

    When complete, a success message displays and a new browser tab opens your website at a temporary cloud URL.

  2. If a success message does not appear, click the updated View! icon on the upper right.
