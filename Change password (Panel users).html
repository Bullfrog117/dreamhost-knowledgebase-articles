Each DreamHost account has a primary email address assigned to it. This email gives you access to the DreamHost panel which you can access at panel.dreamhost.com.

There are two ways to reset this password:

Forgot password link

To reset this password, visit the following link:

  • https://panel.dreamhost.com/id/forgot.cgi

Enter your email address and the system will send you an email with further instructions.

Only the primary email set up on your account can reset your password using this link. If you require other people to access your account you must set up Account Privileges for each new user.

Reset password in the panel

  1. Navigate to the (Panel > 'HOME' > 'Dashboard') page.
  2. On the bottom left, click the 'Edit Profile' link.
  3. Click the 'Security' link.
  4. On this page you can change the password and set a secret question.
