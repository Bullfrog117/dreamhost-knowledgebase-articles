Is Remixer included in my DreamHost plan?

Remixer is included with any shared hosting plan.

Remixer is not included in your VPS-only, DreamPress-only, and Dedicated-only plans. To use Remixer with your VPS or Dedicated plan, you can purchase the Remixer Standard, Remixer All Access, or a shared hosting plan. Remixer does not work with DreamPress.
