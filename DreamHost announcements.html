DreamHost strives to keep customers in the loop of any issues that could possibly affect service. The primary method of announcing system changes and status information is the status page:

  • DreamHost Status Blog

This should be checked regularly for most announcements.

However, DreamHost may still send announcements to customers in order to notify them of upgrades, service outages, and so on.

Can I get announcements emailed to me?

General announcements are displayed on the status blog. Any specific announcements to you show in your support history which are also emailed to the primary email listed on the account.

To make sure you receive any such announcements, click the edit profile link in the panel.

This takes you to your profile page where you can view and/or change your account details. Here you can set your preferences for email notifications from DreamHost.
