How to add an email account to Outlook 2016 on Windows.

  1. Open Outlook 2016 from your start menu.
  2. On the top left, click the 'File' tab.
  3. From the popout menu, click 'Add Account'.
  4. Enter your email address and click 'Connect'.
  5. Enter your password and check the box to save it. Then click 'Ok'.
  6. An SSL warning pops up informing you the SSL mail certificate you're about to connect to cannot be verified. Click 'View Certificate'.
  7. You can then see it's assigned to *.mail.dreamhost.com. The error is because it's not assigned specifically to your domain name. It's assigned to mail.dreamhost.com which is safe to use. Click 'Install Certificate'.
  8. The 'Certificate Import Wizard' pops up. Click the 'Next' button.
  9. Click 'Next' again.
  10. Click 'Finish'.
  11. A popup displays confirming the certificate import succeeded. Click 'OK'.
  12. On the former certificate page (where you can see *.mail.dreamhost.com, click 'OK'.
  13. Finally, on the original popup for the certificate, click the 'Yes' button.
  14. If prompted, enter your password again.
  15. A final page displays confirming the address was added to Outlook 2016.
