As of August 22nd, 2006 the ability to forward email from a DreamHost-hosted email account to AOL was removed.

Forwards set up prior to this system change will still remain active, though no new ones or forwards that are deleted and re-added can be set to forward to AOL addresses.

More information about this change can be read on the following DreamHost status page post.
