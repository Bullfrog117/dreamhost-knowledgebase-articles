The transfer process between DreamHost accounts is very similar to transferring a domain from an external provider. First, the account holder that is releasing the domain must prepare the domain for transfer in their own panel, by following the steps in the Transfer your domain registration away from DreamHost article.

The account holder provides the authorization key/EPP code to the recipient of the domain. The recipient then follows the steps to initiate the transfer in to their account.

Some international domains cannot be transferred from account to account using this process. In these cases, the receiving customerâ€™s panel requests that they contact DreamHost support for further assistance.
