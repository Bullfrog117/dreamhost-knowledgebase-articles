Closing your VPS hosting account

  1. Navigate to the (Panel > ‘VPS’ > ‘Dashboard’) page.
  2. To the right of the VPS under the ‘Actions’ column, click the red Delete button.

    If you have a shared hosting plan or Dedicated hosting server available on the account when you close your VPS, deleting the VPS offloads the user data and domain names to the available service. If there is no other hosting service in place, then the account will simply not have a hosting plan and remain available for domain registrations.

When finished, DreamHost sends the account owner an email to confirm the VPS has been deleted.

Closing your Dedicated hosting account

  1. Navigate to the (Panel > ‘Dedicated Servers’ > ‘Dashboard’) page.
  2. To the right of the server under the ‘Actions’ column, click the red Delete button.

    If you have a shared hosting plan or VPS available on the account when you close your Dedicated server, deleting the Dedicated server offloads the user data and domain names to the available service. If there is no other hosting service in place, then the account will simply not have a hosting plan and remain available for domain registrations.
