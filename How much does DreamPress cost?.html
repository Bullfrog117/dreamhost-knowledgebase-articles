• Pricing is set at $19.95/month/per site. You can also choose a yearly plan which brings down the monthly cost average to $16.95/month.
  • The price is a flat rate and is not affected by VPS memory scaling.
  • You may have multiple DreamPress sites, but they will be billed independently.
  • There are also 'special promotions you can use for a discounted rate. Check out dreamhost.com for any current promo codes. Customers who add a site while the promo is active get to keep the this special price even when the promotion is over. If the site is removed from DreamPress (Downgraded or Deleted), or if the customer's account is closed/disabled, they may lose the special promo pricing.
  • Deleting or downgrading will give a credit for any unused portion of the billing period.
  • If the account is under 97 days and the account is closed completely, as seen on the (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Manage Account’) page, the DreamPress service fee will also get refunded.
