What is a Chord?

After you select a theme, you are ready to edit it. Each theme is divided into sections called 'Chords.' In Remixer, each Chord has a specific layout and design and you can customize any of the content you see within the Chord to fit your needs.

How do I use them?

Chords are used throughout the theme to display your content. You can customize any of the existing data in a Chord.

Below is an inventory and description of each of the available Chords in Remixer.
