After you create a temporary domain for your DreamPress site, you can visit the DreamPress dashboard in the panel to create a new domain and migrate your site to it. You must have at least one DreamPress-enabled domain in order to view and make changes in your dashboard.

You can use the domain name changer in the DreamPress dashboard to change any temporary domain (not just dream.press), even domains that are misspelled.

The domain you migrate TO cannot be hosted. If it is, you must remove hosting for the domain before proceeding. This is so the domain can safely be migrated without breaking a live site.

When you change the domain name, the DreamPress site is migrated to your new site which is now set up on the DreamPress hosting plan.

Changing your domain

To change the DreamPress domain:

  1. Navigate to the (Panel > ‘DreamPress’ > ‘Dashboard’) page.
  2. Click the 'Change' link to the right of a domain.
    The 'Change Domain' dialog box opens:
  3. Enter either an exisiting domain or a new domain, and then click the blue Change domain button to finish.
