This article describes how to publish your Remixer site to a domain.

  1. Click the Pages button in the upper toolbar.
  2. Locate your new page.
  3. Click the gear icon to the right of 'My page'.
    The Page Settings page opens:
  4. If you wish to replace the default 'My page' title, then enter a new title for your page. The title appears on the browser tab when you load your published site.
  5. Select your domain/sub-domain from the dropdown list under 'Publish Destination'.

    If your hostname is currently set up on the DreamHost panel with existing settings such as a fully hosted site or a redirect, it will give you a warning that you already have hosting settings configured and that Remixer will point your hostname away from the existing settings. To proceed, check the box to confirm you understand this.

  6. Click the Publish settings button.
    An animated rocket appears showing you the upload progress of your website:

    When complete, a success message displays and a new browser tab opens your website at a temporary cloud URL.

  7. If a success message does not appear, click the updated View! icon on the upper right.
