Upload video into Remixer

Remixer allows you to upload video from several sources such as social media accounts, Youtube, and Vimeo. Once uploaded, you can add them to your site. To upload:

  1. Click the 'Media' button on the top right.
  2. Click the 'Video' link to view your options to upload.
  3. Click on any option and upload your images.

Editing video

  1. Make sure you've added a 'Chord' section to the page that supports video.
  2. Click the sample video edit:
  3. A video toolbar appears.
  4. You can upload a video you've previously saved on the 'Media' page, search Youtube, or search Vimeo (may be subject to copyright).
