If you refer another customer to DreamHost, you can receive referral rewards.

Which new customers can I receive rewards from?

You can receive rewards from only one type of referred customers:

Primary (Tier-1) Referrals
Customers that have been directly referred to DreamHost by you from a referral link.

How much can I receive for referring new customers?

One-Time Rewards
For each Primary Tier-1 referral, you can earn as much as $97.00.
