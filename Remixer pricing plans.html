Is Remixer inlcuded in my DreamHost plan?

Remixer is not included in your VPS-only, Dreampress-only, and Dedicated-only plans. To add Remixer to one of these existing plan, you can either buy the Remixer-only plan or the Shared/Remixer All Access Plan.
